Overview

The current Internet is built on protocols that were designed nearly 40 years ago and require a hierarchical network with centralized control in order to scale. Technology has progressed exponentially in that time, but the original protocols remain and have not easily adapted to the complexity of today’s networks. Project Meshnet is a clean slate that uses modern technology and ideas to address these shortcomings. Its goal is the creation of a decentralized global network with security, simplicity, and scalability built in from the start.

Powered by cjdns, we already have thousands of nodes running all types of services, but most of these nodes are connected to each other by peering over the regular Internet. We’d like to change that…

Spokane Meshnet is a community of people in the Inland Northwest interested in establishing and maintaining the physical infrastructure to support Project Meshnet’s goals. Combined with other existing “mesh locals”, the Spokane Meshnet embodies this new global network (referred to as “Hyperboria”) that is inherently resistant to censorship, surveillance, and disruption. We’re creating change by creating technology.

Why Should You Care?

Do you care about privacy and upholding civil liberties in a time when it’s increasingly difficult to maintain the confidentiality, authenticity, and integrity of your data online?

Do you desire a resilient network that can stay up even during a natural disaster, local emergency, or (more likely) a construction crew accidentally cutting through a fiber line?

Are you a technology enthusiast who enjoys developing/testing innovative software and tinkering with network equipment?

Do you believe that people should be able to speak out against oppressive regimes, the press should be able to report the truth, and whistleblowers should have a means to expose corruption?

Or are you simply tired of paying your ISP exorbitant amounts money for substandard service?

In all of these cases, Project Meshnet has something to offer you! Much like the Internet circa 1994, we’re a small but helpful community with massive potential. Come and join us.

Get Involved

Spokane Meshnet needs your help. We’re in the early stages of coordinating interested parties, working toward the eventual deployment of wireless point-to-point connections in the region. No matter your technical skill set or background, if you’re interested in the project, please reach out to the group to see how you can help. Consider donating some of your time, your expertise, your voice, or even your roof.

  • Check out the Getting Started Guide
  • Attend one of our in-person meetups to ask questions and get hands-on assistance
  • Install a wireless node at your home or business to expand the network
  • Be a vocal advocate for the project with everyone you know
